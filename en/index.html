About Us

A group of young people from Puglia united by their passion for the sea and the desire to enhance our land. Growing up surrounded by the breathtaking landscapes and crystal-clear waters of Puglia, we decided to create a tool that would simplify the tourist’s experience, improving their seaside vacation while discovering hidden treasures. That’s how PuntoMare was born: a mobile app that, thanks to a proprietary algorithm, offers precise information about sea conditions and forecasts dedicated to bathing, allowing you to choose the perfect spot to enjoy the sea safely every day.

PuntoMare is much more than just a guide: it’s a complete experience for those who love the sea and want to live it to the fullest. In addition to detailed bathing forecasts, the app allows you to discover and book unique experiences, high-quality restaurants, and equipped beach clubs in just a few clicks.
Already available throughout Italy, PuntoMare keeps the same passion and attention to detail that made the Puglia experience truly unforgettable. With us, you can explore the best beaches and destinations in the beautiful country, making your seaside vacation a unique experience.
